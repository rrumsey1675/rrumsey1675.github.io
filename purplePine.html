About Purple Pine

Our mission is to create, for the joy of creation. Purple Pine aims to realize any idea using imagination and innovation.
Established in July 2023, the studio has been developing games, including a gesture-based game for the soon-to-be-released Apple Vision Pro. Additionally, we are creating a few story-driven adventures for various platforms.
