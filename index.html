About Me

Hi, I'm Roisin (pronounced: ro-sheen), a lead developer at the Univerity of Maine's VEMI Lab. Throughout my career, I would like to make an impact with story-based video games. I believe that collaboration is an important step in the creative process. I'm passionate about how we as a society interface with computers and how we can use this medium as a greater art form.
