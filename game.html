Embark on a journey to a not-so-distant future, where Achelous, a newly discovered planet, becomes home to a military outpost. With time, rumors spread of neighboring beings plotting to displace humans from Achelous. A steadfast general, bound by duty, stands resolute, refusing to abandon post. Engage in a story woven from the lives of five distinctive characters, united in their goal of survival. Progress through their paths one by one, guiding their decisions. Will your choices tip the scales and secure their survival?
Penta-gone is a dialogue based 2D side-scroller where the player must play-through 5 different times with 5 different characters before reaching a definitive ending. Each play-through builds upon previous plays and influences the next.
I was inspired to create a game with a level of replayability, and make it so the replays could be just as important as the first time a player explores this world.
