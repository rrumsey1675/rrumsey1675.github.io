In the not too distant future, a military base is built on the newly discovered planet, Achelous. But, ten years leter, when rumors spread about neighboring Beings making an effort to remove the humans from Achelous, and the new General refuses to leave, how will 5 strangers manage to ensure the survival of the Acheloids?
Penta-gone is a dialogue based 2D side-scroller where the player must play-through 5 different times with 5 different characters before reaching a definative ending. Each play-through builds upon previous plays and influences the next.
I was inspired to create a game with a level of replayability, and make it so the replays could be just as important as the first time a player explores this world.
